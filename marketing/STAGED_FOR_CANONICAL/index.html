About

An Ever-Expanding Dark-Fantasy World

Neverending Narratives is a Nashville-based team building the Sorcerer-Sword — a single, connected world you can play, hear, and read. The browser ARPG, the narrated audiobooks, and the novels all tell different sides of the same story.

Everything starts free: play instantly in your browser, stream the opening chapters on YouTube, and read the serial on Royal Road. When you want more, the audiobook bundle and your support keep the world growing — new heroes, new art, new chapters.
