# 13 — Assets to Purchase (earmarked for Kickstarter funds)

A running wishlist of **paid** assets/tools that would raise quality, deliberately deferred until the Kickstarter is funded. **Current rule: free / CC0 assets only.** Nothing here is bought now; this is the shopping list for "once the money is in."

When something here gets bought, move it to `12-asset-license-register.md` with its license + receipt.

## Why this list exists
The 3D remake is being built entirely on free assets (KayKit CC0, Three.js MIT, procedural code, Mixamo free tier). That's enough to recreate the whole game and make a strong trailer. These purchases are the *polish ceiling* the campaign funds — they make it look bought-and-professional rather than scrappy.

## Verify-before-buying checklist
- Commercial license explicitly allows shipping in a paid/crowdfunded game.
- For commissioned work: written rights transfer / work-for-hire.
- For AI-generated assets: confirm the tool grants commercial ownership of outputs.
- Keep every receipt + license in a folder beside the register (`12`).

> Maintenance: whenever you hit a "this would look better if we paid for X" moment during the build, add a row here instead of buying. The Kickstarter budget section (built at bucket B7) will draw directly from this list.
